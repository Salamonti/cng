Impression
Sally Belding presents with persistent respiratory symptoms and productive cough despite recent treatment with antibiotics and steroids. The recent positive Influenza A test indicates a viral component to her symptoms. Her history of EGPA and asthma complicates the clinical picture, and the lack of improvement with current treatments raises concern for possible bacterial superinfection or inadequate control of her underlying conditions.
